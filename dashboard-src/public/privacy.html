Tactus — Privacy Policy

Tactus is an internal financial dashboard built for Tarbet Education Network. It is not a public product and is not distributed to or used by anyone outside the company's own staff.

What data it accesses

Tactus connects to Stripe, PayPal, and QuickBooks Online using credentials configured by Tarbet Education Network, and reads revenue, subscription, and profit & loss data from those accounts.

What it does with that data

Data is displayed only on the internal dashboard, which is password-protected and accessible only to authorized company staff. It is not shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party.

What it stores

QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored in a private Supabase database solely to maintain the connection between Tactus and the company's own QuickBooks account. No customer or end-user personal data is collected, stored, or processed by Tactus.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to Tarbet Education Network.
